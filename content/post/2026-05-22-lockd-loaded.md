  of this until I [read][infoworld] about the limits Anthropic is putting on
  Claude. In my workflow, using Claude Code via Zed Terminal Thread will be the
  only way to continue billing at the subscription price instead of volume
  price.

- [The Boy That Cried Mythos][mythos] - Speaking of Claude, this was a poignant

  impending model.
